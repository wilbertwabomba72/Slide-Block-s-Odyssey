Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Slide Block's Odyssey handles information when you use the game on iPhone. Slide Block's Odyssey is designed as a fully offline single-player puzzle experience.

Effective date: April 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Slide Block's Odyssey does not require account creation and does not ask you to submit personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location during normal gameplay.

The game stores gameplay-related data locally on your device, including:

  • Highest unlocked level
  • Best move counts for completed levels
  • Star ratings and local progress totals
  • Unlocked themes and selected theme
  • Music and sound effect settings

2. How Information Is Used

Local gameplay data is used only to provide the core game experience, restore your progress, and remember your settings on the device where you play.

3. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

Slide Block's Odyssey does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking tools. The game does not use your data for behavioral advertising and does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

4. Data Sharing

Slide Block's Odyssey does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties because the app is not designed to collect personal data for that purpose.

5. Internet and Network Activity

Normal gameplay is intended to work without an internet connection. The app does not rely on user accounts, external game servers, or cloud gameplay services to function.

6. Data Storage and Retention

Gameplay data is stored locally on your device through iOS system storage. If you remove the app, locally stored game data may be deleted from the device. Device backups, if any, are managed by Apple and your own iOS settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Slide Block's Odyssey is designed as a general-audience puzzle game. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children through in-app forms, account systems, or similar data entry features.

8. Your Choices

Because gameplay data is stored locally, you can reset your local progress by deleting the app and its local data from your device, subject to your iOS backup and restore settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s features or data practices change. The latest version should be published with the app materials or support pages associated with Slide Block's Odyssey.

10. Contact

If you have privacy questions about Slide Block's Odyssey, please contact the developer by email at hengbozail@163.com.

Summary: Slide Block's Odyssey is an offline puzzle game that stores gameplay progress locally on the device and does not include ad tracking, account systems, or third-party analytics in its current implementation.
