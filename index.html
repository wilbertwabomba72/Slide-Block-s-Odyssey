Offline iPhone Puzzle

Slide Block's Odyssey

Slide Block's Odyssey is a polished single-player sliding block puzzle for iPhone. Move surrounding blocks, clear the lane, and guide the red block to the exit across fifty handcrafted levels built for pure, repeatable logic play.

Every stage has a fixed layout and a known optimum solution. Progress comes from planning better moves, learning cleaner routes, and replaying for exact clears instead of relying on random generation.

Why It Plays Well

Slide Block's Odyssey is built in SpriteKit with Objective-C and tuned for fast portrait play, readable interfaces, smooth block movement, and fully local progress.

Classic Rules

Horizontal blocks move left and right, vertical blocks move up and down, and the target block must reach the right-side exit. The rules stay clear, immediate, and easy to understand from the first level.

Exact Optimum Goals

Each level includes a known optimum move count. Three-star clears require an exact match, making every replay meaningful for players who enjoy precision.

Pure Offline Progress

The game is designed for normal play without internet access, accounts, cloud sync, or live services. Your level progress, best moves, theme unlocks, and audio settings are stored locally on the device.

What You Get

The structure is intentionally focused: fixed content, readable difficulty progression, and clean replay loops instead of endless procedural filler.

Core Package

  • Fifty handcrafted single-player levels split across five pages.
  • Board sizes that vary across the campaign for broader puzzle rhythm.
  • Fast restart and direct replay support for move optimization.

Progress Systems

  • Best move tracking per level.
  • Three-star clear ratings and cumulative progress feedback.
  • Theme unlock milestones tied to perfect clears on Levels 10, 20, 30, and 40.

Player Experience

  • Portrait interface tuned for iPhone.
  • Simple tap-and-drag controls with immediate feedback.
  • Quick sessions for short breaks or longer mastery runs.
